About HealthBot

HealthBot is an AI-driven public health assistant designed to provide instant guidance, disease awareness, and health tips. It integrates trusted sources like WHO and CDC to give reliable information to users anywhere, anytime.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered health guidance and chat support
  • 24/7 availability for immediate assistance
  • Disease prevention and awareness tips
  • Trusted information based on global health standards
